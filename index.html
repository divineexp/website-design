Frequently Asked

If your question is not addressed below, bring it to the discovery call.

Why do you build exclusively on Squarespace?
Building on a single platform allows us to develop a depth of expertise that generalist agencies cannot match. We know Squarespace completely — its capabilities, its constraints, and how to build within it at a level that produces clean, performant, and maintainable results. That specialization directly benefits the quality, timeline, and longevity of your project.
What does copywriting included actually mean?
We produce a complete first draft of every page's copy before design begins — headlines, body content, calls to action, and supporting text. That draft is produced with AI assistance and refined by our team to align with your voice, your offer, and your audience. You review, provide notes, and we finalize. No page goes into design without approved copy.
Do I need to have my brand identity finalized before we begin?
Yes. We apply your brand — we do not create it. A finalized logo, color system, and typography are required before the project begins. If those assets are not yet in place, we will identify that in the strategy session and advise on the appropriate next steps before any work commences.
What if my requirements exceed the package I've selected?
Scope is confirmed at the strategy session before any work begins. If your requirements are better suited to a higher tier, we communicate that clearly at the outset. Any additions beyond confirmed scope are priced separately and require written approval before being incorporated.
Can you redesign an existing Squarespace site?
Yes. Redesigns follow the same package structure. We conduct a full audit during the strategy session, determine what carries forward and what is rebuilt from the ground up, and finalize scope accordingly.
What are the payment terms?
Fifty percent is due at project commencement, with the remaining fifty percent due prior to final delivery. We accept card and ACH. Payment plans are available for projects above $2,500 — please raise this during the discovery call.
What support is available after the site launches?
Every project includes fourteen days of post-launch support for corrections and minor adjustments at no additional cost. You also receive a full recorded walkthrough and complete editor access at handoff, enabling you to manage the site independently beyond the support window.
